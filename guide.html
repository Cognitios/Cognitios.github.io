Introduction

Welcome to Cognitios, this is a continuation of the previous Cognitios by the same author of the original, only revamped. Upon the success of Cognitios, I realized that there needs to be something more than aggregating links in order to educate people; I was just providing a library with some really bad books to learn from and no structure. Google does a better job of that; so instead, I intend to create a curriculum based on a mix of online and offline resources.

If you have any suggestions for this website, please email me at cognitios@teknik.io, or you can push a commit if you know how to use git.

I plan on making this my own website in the near future depending on the success of this test run. There isn't a point to making my own domain name at this point; all I'm providing is a few frontend guides.

Finally, I am not an expert on any of these fields; I consult people who are experts and some anonymous people on the internet. I have aggregated some guides posted by anonymous people and I would like to thank those people here for the work they do not for recognition or self gratification, but because they just want to see the world learn.
