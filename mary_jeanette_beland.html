Mary Jeanette "Julie" Beland

1927 -

Mary Jeanette "Julie" Beland was born on September 10th, 1927 in St. Johnsbury, Vermont [1]. She lived in Vermont until April 10th of 1935 when she moved to Lennoxville Quebec.
